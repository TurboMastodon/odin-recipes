Scrambled Eggs

Are you ready to make some delicious scrambled eggs?

In this recipe, you will find joy in making world-renowned scrambled eggs!

Ingredients

  • Skillet
  • Spatula
  • Bowl
  • Fork
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Freshly-Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Get a skillet and place it on the eye of your stove.
  2. Turn the eye to medium heat.
  3. Crack two eggs into the bowl and beat until well mixed with fork.
  4. Put a knob of butter in the skillet.
  5. Once butter is melted, move it around the inside of the skillet to coat it.
  6. Put egg mixture into skillet, and start stirring the eggs. Do not stop stirring until eggs begin to curdle.
  7. Once the mixture looks solidified, place egg on a plate, and enjoy!
